Simple

We keep it simple and smooth. Our app finds the best trucks based on location and line congesion. So you can find us, drink up, and get on the go.

Sustainable

Electric trucks. Biodegradeable plastic cups. Sustainably sourced ingredients. We don't just talk sustainability, we live by it.

Superfoods

Adaptogenic herbs, goji berries, plant-based milks, nut proteins. Our varied selection fuels the most studious students and the most driven gym junkies. It's not called super for nothing.

Supportive

Students are the future. That's why we work directly with colleges to provide students with quick and easy smoothies. We even deliver to dorms, free of charge, using starship robots.
