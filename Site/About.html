About Us

We are a Dallas-based juice food truck. Each of our trucks is stocked daily with juices made from fresh and locally sourced ingredients. Our founders created this company when they were seniors in college, and aim to provide healthy drinks on a budget. You can located us roaming around Dallas on our Home page.

Our Mission

With our passion for the environment and our committment to making affordable juices available to everyone, we've pledged to donate 1% of all proceeds to environmental non-profit organizations.
